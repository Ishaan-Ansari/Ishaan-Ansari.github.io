Ishaan Ansari

I am a Software Engineer in AI, building production-grade systems for enterprises at scale, My work sits at the intersection of computer vision and language modelling.

I graduated in 2023 with a B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering with Honors. As an undergraduate, I worked on computer vision for medical imaging, building early intuition about the brittleness of discriminative models and the importance of interpretability in open-world clinical settings.

My eventual goal is to help build AI that reason over dynamics, that maintain internal models of how the world evolves, simulate futures, and act over long time horizons without human scaffolding at every step.
